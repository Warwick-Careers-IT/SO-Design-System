Job search advice appointments

Our job search advisers & careers project officers support students and graduates with:

  • Writing and targeting CVs
  • Searching for jobs
  • Applications
  • Interviews
  • Work experience

Bookable job search advice takes place in University House

Job search advice appointments are 20 minutes long and cover the topics above. If you require more indepth discussions around career planning and career choices please make an appointment with a careers consultant

What we expect from you

  • Honour any commitments that you have made with us; turn up on time for the start of events or workshops you have signed up to. Failure to attend two events or appointments will result in temporary withdrawal of bookable services for 4 weeks
  • Have a realistic view of what we can discuss with you during the time available during the 20 minutes. If you arrive late then your appointment will be shortened accordingly
  • If you want to discuss your CV or an application please bring a copy to your appointment
