Targeting your CV

Do your research about the company/role BEFORE you write your CV so you are clear what skills and experience they are looking for you to demonstrate

Make sure you tailor your CV for each role you are applying for – there is no such thing as a generic CV.

General tips

  • Tailor your CV to the organisation.
  • Prioritise the most relevant, important information.
  • Make sure you tailor your CV for each role you are applying for.
